Shut the Box

Rules:

Shut the Box is a traditional pub game. The objective is to close all the numbered tiles by rolling dice and selecting combinations of tiles that match the total of the dice roll.

On each turn, roll the dice and select any combination of open tiles that adds up to the total of the dice. After confirming your selection, the chosen tiles will be closed and no longer available for the rest of the game.

The game continues until you successfully close all the tiles or are unable to make a valid combination.

Scoring: Your score is the sum of the closed tile numbers when the game ends.
